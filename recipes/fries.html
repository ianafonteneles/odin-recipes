Fries Recipe

Description

A thin strip of potato, usually cut 3 to 4 inches in length and about 1/4 to 3/8 inches square that are deep fried until they are golden brown and crisp textured on the outside while remaining white and soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium (1kg) sebago or red delight potatoes, peeled vegetable oil, for deep-frying
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp cracked black pepper
  • Alioli, to serve

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes
  2. Soak them cold in water for at least an hour or overnight.
  3. Rinse them twice with cold water and pat the completely dry.
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees.
  5. Increase heat to 400 degrees
  6. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle immediately with salt. Serve with ketchup!
